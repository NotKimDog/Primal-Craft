## üèÉ Stamina System

### Overview
Advanced stamina mechanic that manages player energy and affects sprint capability.

### Features
- **Sprint Management** - Limited sprint duration based on stamina level
- **Sprint Cooldown** - Recovery time after stamina depletes
- **Visual Indicator** - HUD bar showing current stamina
- **Stamina Recovery** - Regenerates while standing still or sleeping
- **Combat Integration** - Affects swing attack effectiveness

### How it Works
1. Sprint depletes stamina bar
2. When depleted, sprint becomes unavailable
3. Stamina slowly recovers
4. Sleeping restores stamina fully
5. Rest for 5+ seconds to accelerate recovery

### Tips
- Rest periodically during exploration
- Sleep at night to fully restore stamina
- Build shelter for safe rest spots
- Manage stamina on long expeditions
